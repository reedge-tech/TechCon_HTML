History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local meetup for developers and has since grown into an internationally recognized event attracting thousands of tech enthusiasts each year.

Mission

Our mission is to empower innovation, share emerging trends, and build a strong, inclusive tech community across the globe. TechCon 2024 focuses on ethical technology, sustainability, and empowering youth in tech.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the brightest minds in the industry:

  • Tim Berners-Lee – Inventor of the World Wide Web, advocate for internet freedom and data privacy.

  • Sheryl Sandberg – COO of Facebook (Meta), champion of women in tech leadership.
